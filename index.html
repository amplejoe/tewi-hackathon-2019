Research Demo Hackathon

The goal of the hackathon is to (i) find a team with which you can (ii) define a research demo application which you can publish at a scientific conference, and then (iii) implement it in two days time. It’s actually big fun and it gives you the opportunity to learn to knew new people, learn new things ... and publish a new paper.

Who is invited? PhD students, post docs, researchers, teachers, professors, etc.
Where and When? 21. to 22. of December, 2016 / AAU Klagenfurt
Why should we do that? Because it’s fun, and you can meet your co-workers, and it is a great way to add a finishing line to the year 2016, and you can make contacts, and you can publish the results, and and and ...
