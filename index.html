Tewi Hackathon '19

The goal of the hackathon is to investigate the topic of reproducibility by

(i) collecting research papers from various fields

(ii) identifying good and bad practices

(iii) have fun working in small teams toward a common goal

Who is invited? PhD students, post docs, researchers, teachers, professors, etc.
Where and When? 18. to 19. of March, 2019 / AAU Klagenfurt
Why should we do that? Because it’s fun, and you can meet your co-workers, and it is a great way to concern yourself with interesting research of potentially unfamiliar areas, and you can make contacts, and you can publish the results, and and and ...
